The wing has three distinct activities presently:

1. Regular Discussion: Since we are a critical thinking group, we talk among ourselves for an increased awareness on various issues.If there is an issue that is queer, or can be queered, rest assured that We shall talk about it.You may walk into any of our meetings and contribute your views.These meetings are held weekly and the information about the next one can be found on our page.You may access the minutes on the blog.

2. Community Interaction: We cannot work in isolation from the community. Often, we take a camera and notepad,reach out to people and talk to them about gender and sexuality issues they face.We also host various events that invite community voices.

3. Social Outreach:  We have a community page that discusses group activities, shares readings and resources and keeps community member informed about our events. We also reach out to similar for across the country to share the ideas,resources and discuss events.
