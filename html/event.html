Archive of events Yearwise :

2016 :

1. Talk Gender: july 28 - August 11 , 2016 :

This competition was modeled along the Tiny Tales format, and invited entries from the campus population. We saw various entries which were published through our blog on the page.

2. Un/acceptable: October 1 , 2016 :

We collaborated with the English Literary Society to host a Poetry Slam. The theme for the event was gender stereotypes in relationships. We discussed the types of relationships that are allowed in the society and the types that are not. The session saw many poems, which have been shared through our digital spaces.

3. The Pink Campus: October 2, 2016 :

We collaborated with the Debating Society to host a discussion on sexism in IIT Kanpur. We saw a participation of over 50 people and we covered a lot of issues. The minutes for the meeting are available on our blog and are being shared through our page.

4. Gender Sessions:

We collaborated with Prayas to organize gender sessions for the children. The sessions covered the topics of identity, stereotypes, body, menstruation and harassment.

2017 :

1. International Day Against Homophobia, Biphobia and Transphobia (IDAHBT) :

May 18 We organized a social media campaign on IDAHBT-2017 , where we posted a pledge through our Facebook page and asked students to share it widely through their friends and acquaintances. The campaign was a success and we got a huge response.

2. Queer Pe Charcha: June 1 - June 26, 2017 :

June was Pride Month and we organized Queer Pe Charcha , a social awareness campaign on all things queer, in association with seven similar groups across academic institutes nationwide. Each day, from June 1 - June 26, we put up an infographic on definitions, myths and resources related to these issues.
